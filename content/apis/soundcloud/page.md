SoundCloud API provides access to the world's largest open audio platform. Integrate music streaming, discover tracks, manage playlists, and build audio-powered applications with millions of tracks from independent artists and labels.

### What you can build

- Music streaming apps
- Playlist curators
- Artist discovery platforms
- Audio analytics tools
- Podcast players
- Music recommendation engines
- Social music networks
- DJ mixing applications
