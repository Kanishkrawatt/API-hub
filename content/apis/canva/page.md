Canva API enables developers to integrate Canva's powerful design platform into their applications. Build design tools, automate visual content creation, and leverage Canva's templates and assets for programmatic design generation.

### What you can build

- Design automation tools
- Template-based content generators
- Social media post schedulers
- Brand asset management systems
- Marketing collateral creators
- Personalized design tools
- Bulk image generation systems
- White-label design platforms
