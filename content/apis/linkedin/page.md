LinkedIn API enables integration with the world's largest professional network of over 900 million members. Build career tools, recruitment platforms, professional networking features, and marketing solutions leveraging LinkedIn's professional graph.

### What you can build

- Professional networking applications
- Job board and recruitment platforms
- Social media management tools
- Professional profile analyzers
- Career development applications
- B2B lead generation tools
- Employee advocacy platforms
- Professional content sharing tools
