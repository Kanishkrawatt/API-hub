Medusa API provides complete access to a modern, open-source headless commerce platform. Build flexible, customizable e-commerce experiences with full control over products, checkout, payments, and customer management.

### What you can build

- Custom online stores
- Multi-channel commerce (web, mobile, IoT)
- Marketplace platforms
- Subscription commerce
- B2B e-commerce portals
- Headless storefronts
- Commerce integrations
- Custom checkout experiences
