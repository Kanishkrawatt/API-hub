Calendly API enables programmatic access to the popular scheduling platform used by millions. Automate meeting bookings, integrate scheduling into your applications, and build custom workflows around appointment management.

### What you can build

- Embedded scheduling widgets
- CRM scheduling integrations
- Automated booking systems
- Custom availability managers
- Meeting analytics dashboards
- Sales workflow automation
- Support ticket scheduling
- Team calendar coordinators
