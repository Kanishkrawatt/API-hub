Perplexity API provides access to AI-powered search and answer generation with real-time web knowledge. Unlike traditional LLMs, Perplexity specializes in grounding responses with current information and providing citations, making it ideal for research and fact-based applications.

### What you can build

- AI-powered search engines
- Research assistants with source citations
- Fact-checking and verification tools
- Question-answering systems with references
- News aggregation and analysis tools
- Academic research helpers
- Customer support with up-to-date product info
